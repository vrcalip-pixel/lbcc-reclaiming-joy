The Short Version

AI is changing the classroom — fast. Agentic AI can now log into Canvas and submit assignments for students. Many of us are feeling the weight of it. This event is not about surveillance or detection. It's about returning to joy, human connection, and the parts of teaching that no AI can replicate.

"The best defense against automation isn't tighter surveillance — it's returning to what machines cannot do: witness authentic growth, honor cultural knowledge, and create learning where students show up as their full selves."

— Event Description, Reclaiming Joy 2026

What You'll Walk Away With

Learning Outcomes

  • Strategies to make student thinking visible — without relying on detection tools

  • Practical ways to protect transfer integrity and promote equity
  • A renewed sense of creative energy and community with colleagues statewide
